WHAT IS LIBRAL ARTS?

It is difficult to feel connected to our communities when the spaces where we are celebrated have been compromised by the violence of war and the weaponization of ignorance.

In response, we envisioned a new kind of immersive online environment that would become a familiar companion over time, like visiting a good friend, or your favorite establishment.

Within this site, we have created an oasis of worlds for you to explore–an online safe space that anyone can access from anywhere–to replenish just a little of what we have collectively lost.

NINE TO FIVELESS

Failure is an opportunity for growth. The path to learning is more often than not blocked by our own fear of admitting what we don't know. In this space we celebrate the things we don't understand by revealing the bumpy path to ingest the information itself and addressing the reasons we felt uncomfortable that we didn't have these answers in the first place.

If we are brave enough to fail, then there is no limit to what we can learn.
